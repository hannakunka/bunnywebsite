Sound

You might think rabbits are quiet pets. But if you listen, you’ll find that a bunny seems to have a noise or sound for every occasion. Rabbit noises have very specific meanings. So listen closely. The sound start with one big bunny sneeze at the beginning. The sound that I created is a great getaway to the imaginary forest. My voice over talks about the rabbits and where they feel at their best. We can hear the birds chirping, rabbits jumping, sneezing and eating in the background. There is also sound of a bell from large rabbit which gives the sounds a happy vibration. A rabbit’s purr is different from a cat’s purr. It’s softer, and the rabbit makes the noise by rubbing its teeth together rather than vibrating the throat muscles. Purring can be a sign of contentment which we can also hear in the sound.
